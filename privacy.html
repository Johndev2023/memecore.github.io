# MemeCore Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** July 22, 2025 --- ## 1. Introduction Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MemeCore ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects data when you use our Discord bot. By using MemeCore, you consent to the practices described below. --- ## 2. What Data We Collect We collect only the minimal data required for the bot to function: - **Server Information**: Server ID, server name, and server settings. - **User Identifiers**: User ID and command usage data. - **Message Content**: Only if a command specifically requires message content (e.g., for moderation logs), and only if configured by server admins. We do **NOT** collect or store: - Direct messages - Email addresses - Personal or financial information --- ## 3. How We Use Your Data Data is used to: - Execute bot features and commands - Improve bot performance and reliability - Identify abuse or misuse of the bot - Provide moderation logging (if enabled by your server) --- ## 4. Data Sharing We do **not** sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties. Data may be shared only: - To comply with legal obligations - To respond to abuse reports or terms violations --- ## 5. Data Storage & Security All data is stored securely and only accessible to the bot developer. Reasonable precautions are taken to prevent unauthorized access. You can request deletion of your server’s data by contacting us via the [Support Server](https://discord.gg/8zKWzrmPbT). --- ## 6. User Control You may remove MemeCore from your server at any time to stop further data collection. You may also request a copy or deletion of your stored data. To make a request, please open a ticket in our [Support Server](https://discord.gg/8zKWzrmPbT). --- ## 7. Updates to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page, with a new effective date. --- ## 8. Contact For questions, concerns, or data requests, please contact the bot developer through the [Support Server](https://discord.gg/8zKWzrmPbT). --- © 2025 MemeCore. All rights reserved.
